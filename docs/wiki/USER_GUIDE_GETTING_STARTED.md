## üîê Step 1: Choose Your Encryption Tier

**This is the most important decision - it cannot be changed later!**

### Option A: E2E (End-to-End Encrypted) - Maximum Privacy

**Choose E2E if you:**
- Value absolute privacy above all else
- Handle sensitive or confidential information
- Don't need advanced search or AI features
- Are comfortable with manual device setup
- Work in journalism, activism, or sensitive fields

**E2E Features:**
- ‚úÖ Your private keys never leave your device
- ‚úÖ Server cannot decrypt your content under any circumstances
- ‚úÖ Government-grade encryption (X25519 + AES-256-GCM)
- ‚úÖ Perfect for sensitive content
- ‚ùå Limited search capabilities
- ‚ùå Manual device setup required
- ‚ùå No AI features or smart suggestions

### Option B: UCE (User-Controlled Encryption) - Smart Features

**Choose UCE if you:**
- Want convenience with security
- Use multiple devices frequently
- Need powerful search across all entries
- Want AI-powered features and suggestions
- Prefer easy account recovery options

**UCE Features:**
- ‚úÖ Full-text search across all entries
- ‚úÖ AI-powered auto-tagging
- ‚úÖ Easy multi-device sync
- ‚úÖ Email-based account recovery
- ‚úÖ Advanced social media features
- üîí Content encrypted at rest on server
- üîí Server can only decrypt with your password

## üìù Step 2: Create Your Account

### For E2E Users:
1. **Visit the signup page**
2. **Enter your email and create a strong password**
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
3. **Select "E2E - Maximum Privacy"**
4. **Save your recovery codes** ‚ö†Ô∏è **CRITICAL STEP**
   - You'll receive 10 recovery codes
   - These are the ONLY way to recover your account
   - Write them down on paper and store safely
   - Consider storing copies in multiple secure locations
5. **Complete account creation**

### For UCE Users:
1. **Visit the signup page**
2. **Enter your email and create a strong password**
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
3. **Select "UCE - Smart Features"**
4. **Verify your email address**
5. **Account created - you're ready to start!**

## ‚úçÔ∏è Step 3: Create Your First Entry

### Web Application:
1. **Click the "+" button** or "Create Entry"
2. **Add a title** (optional but recommended)
3. **Write your entry** in the text area
4. **Add tags** to categorize your entry
   - Type tags separated by commas
   - UCE users get auto-suggestions
5. **Add media** (photos/videos) by clicking the media button
6. **Click "Save"** - your entry is automatically encrypted

### Mobile Apps:
1. **Tap the "+" floating action button**
2. **Add a title** (optional)
3. **Write your entry** using the text editor
4. **Add tags** using the tag input
5. **Add photos/videos** using the camera or gallery button
6. **Tap "Save"** - encrypted and synced automatically

## üì± Step 4: Set Up Additional Devices (Optional)

### For E2E Users:
1. **Install the app** on your new device
2. **Tap "Log in to existing account"**
3. **Enter your email and password**
4. **Use a recovery code** when prompted
   - Each code can only be used once
   - Your entries will sync to the new device
5. **Save the remaining recovery codes** safely

### For UCE Users:
1. **Install the app** on your new device
2. **Tap "Log in to existing account"**
3. **Enter your email and password**
4. **Your entries automatically sync** to the new device

## üîß Step 5: Configure Your Settings

### Security Settings:
- **Enable biometric authentication** (mobile apps)
  - Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint
  - Adds an extra layer of security
- **Set app lock timer** (how long before re-authentication required)
- **Review recovery options** (E2E: recovery codes, UCE: email)

### Sync Settings:
- **Auto-sync frequency** (how often to sync when online)
- **Sync on cellular** (enable/disable to save data)
- **Background sync** (sync when app is not active)

### Privacy Settings:
- **Default entry privacy** (private, friends, public)
- **Social media sharing defaults**
- **Data export options**

## üì≤ Step 6: Connect Social Media (Optional)

## üÜò Getting Help

### Common Issues:
- **Forgot password**: Use account recovery (UCE) or recovery codes (E2E)
- **App won't sync**: Check internet connection and try manual sync
- **Can't log in**: Verify email and password, check caps lock
- **Recovery code not working**: Ensure you're typing it exactly as shown

### Support Channels:
- **In-app help**: Tap the "?" icon in settings
- **Email support**: support@diary.xmojo.net
- **Documentation**: Complete guides in this wiki
- **Community**: Discord server and Reddit community

### Troubleshooting:
- **Try logging out and back in**
- **Check for app updates**
- **Restart the app**
- **Clear cache** (Android: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Personal Diary ‚Üí Storage)
- **Reinstall the app** (last resort - E2E users need recovery codes!)
