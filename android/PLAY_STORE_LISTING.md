### Encryption Tiers Explained

**End-to-End Encrypted (E2E) - Maximum Security**
Perfect for: Deeply personal thoughts, sensitive information
- Your device generates encryption keys
- Server never sees unencrypted content
- Only you can decrypt your entries
- Requires password for recovery

**User-Controlled Encryption (UCE) - Balanced Security**
Perfect for: Daily journaling with strong protection
- Argon2 password hashing (used by password managers)
- Strong encryption with user-controlled keys
- Balance between security and convenience

**Standard - Secure Cloud Backup**
Perfect for: Casual journaling with cloud convenience
- Server-side encryption
- Easy recovery and access
- Secure, but server has encryption keys

### FAQ
Q: Is my diary really private?
A: Yes! With E2E encryption, only you can read your entries. We never have access to your unencrypted content.

Q: What happens if I forget my password?
A: You'll receive recovery codes during signup. Keep them safe - they're the only way to recover your account.

Q: Can I export my diary?
A: Yes! You can export your entries anytime in encrypted or plain text format.

Q: Is there a subscription fee?
A: The app is free with optional premium features (future).

Q: Which encryption should I choose?
A: E2E for maximum security, UCE for balanced protection, Standard for convenience.
